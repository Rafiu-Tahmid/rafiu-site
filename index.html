I'm Rafiu — IBA student, CFA Level 1 passer, and someone trying to build a better life while juggling more than I probably should.

Everything I make is about what actually helps an ambitious young Bangladeshi move forward — productivity, AI tools, motivation, personal finance, and the future of work. Not theory. Not generic advice. What I'm actually researching, testing, and living right now.

I'm not an expert. I'm a student of all of this — and I share everything I learn, as I learn it.

Everything connects back to one question: how does someone with limited time, limited money, and unlimited pressure actually move forward? I research obsessively — academic studies, the best books, Bangladesh-specific data — and share honestly, in our context. Shariah-compliant options always sit alongside the conventional ones.

Some background: I've won two finance competitions, cleared CFA Level 1, and completed 30+ courses across programming and finance. All of it feeds directly into what I make — so when I say something is researched, it is.

Currently building toward CFA Level 2 (May 2027) while running the channel and finishing my degree. Everything I talk about, I'm living simultaneously.
